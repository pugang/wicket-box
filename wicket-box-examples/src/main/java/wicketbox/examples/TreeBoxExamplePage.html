TreeBox combines three different behaviors to enhance a standard Wicket TableTree:

Scroll
The horizontal scroll position is synchronized between the header and the body of the tree, by default it is persisted in the document (i.e. the scroll position is kept across ajax updates)
ColResize
Columns are resizable, by default the widths are persisted in a cookie
Stretch
The tree's body stretches to the available height, the footer is always attached to the bottom
